About Us

Phoenix Travel & Tour is a private travel agencey helping people to fulfill their dreams of travelling around the world. Our agencey was first established in 2001. A total of 12 individuals and 4 groups were serviced our agencey in its opening year. But now the number has soared up to 110+ people and 60+ groups per year. We provide many destinations popular among travellers along with well-planned trips. Every members of our team are professional at their work. The customers are very satisfied with the service provided and urge to travel more often with our agencey. The travel agents are very professional and experienced and are also skilled in various languages hence helping to communicate with people across the world. We allow our costomers to experience various adventures such as rafting, paragliding, trekking, jungle safari and many other activities. We also plan trips which are friendly towards the children and elderly people. You can be assured that travelling with Phoenix will surely be very great experience.
